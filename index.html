Days 0 - 30

Listen before acting

  • Individual meetings with all sales team members - understand the real terrain, current pipeline, and key account dynamics
  • Deep dives with top-performing accounts - what is working, what has been promised, where the growth potential sits
  • Alignment with UK Group teams on France KPIs, reporting expectations, and strategic priorities
  • Full pipeline audit: conversion rates by brand, segment, and sales rep - identify where the gaps are
Days 30 - 60

Stabilise and prioritise

  • Install a clear performance framework - weekly pipeline reviews, forecast discipline, individual accountability
  • Identify the top 20% of accounts with highest upsell and cross-brand potential - build dedicated action plans
  • Launch targeted outbound push in 2-3 priority segments - enterprise, scale-up, and professional services
  • First B2B networking activations - business communities, chambers of commerce, sector events
Days 60 - 90

Build for growth

  • Present France 2026-2027 commercial plan to leadership: growth levers, team structure, channel priorities
  • CRM, reporting, and shared forecast fully operational and aligned with Group
  • First measurable results on pipeline conversion and account development visible
  • Team operating with a clear performance culture - each rep knows their number and their plan
